# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity,
gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal
appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

**Expected behaviour:**
- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Accept feedback gracefully and offer it kindly
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users

**Unacceptable behaviour:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks in any form
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Sustained disruptive behaviour in issues, PRs, or discussions

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported to **elar.muzik@gmail.com**.
All reports will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response
deemed appropriate to the circumstances.
